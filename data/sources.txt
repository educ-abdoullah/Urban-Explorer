I'll outline the Gold-layer outputs and transformations that fit your project.## Gold layer: what to do

In your project, the **Gold** layer should contain **business-ready tables** for analysis, scoring, and visualization.

Silver = cleaned data  
Gold = **useful indicators**

---

## What you should do in Gold

### 1. **Aggregate by geographic unit**
Choose the final level of analysis:
- **arrondissement**
- or **quartier** if you have that mapping later

Then compute metrics per zone:
- number of pharmacies
- number of schools
- number of hospitals
- number of sports facilities
- surface of green spaces
- average noise level
- population totals

---

### 2. **Create thematic indicators**
Build one score/table per theme, for example:

- `gold_health`
  - pharmacies count
  - hospitals count

- `gold_education`
  - schools / collèges / lycées count

- `gold_sport`
  - sports facilities count

- `gold_environment`
  - green space surface
  - parks count
  - noise penalty

- `gold_population`
  - total population
  - age-group shares

---

### 3. **Join everything into one final table**
Create one final dataset like:

```text
gold_pafs_by_arrondissement
```

with columns such as:
- `arrondissement`
- `population_total`
- `pharmacies_count`
- `schools_count`
- `hospitals_count`
- `sports_count`
- `green_area_m2`
- `avg_noise_db`

This becomes your main table for:
- dashboards
- maps
- ranking neighborhoods
- profile-based scoring

---

### 4. **Normalize indicators**
Because variables are on different scales, normalize them:
- min-max scaling
- z-score
- ranking percentile

Example:
- pharmacies count: 0–1
- green area: 0–1
- noise: reverse score (less noise = better)

---

### 5. **Compute the final PAFS scores**
This is the key Gold task.

You can create:
- `score_health`
- `score_education`
- `score_sport`
- `score_environment`
- `score_noise`

then combine them into:
- `score_senior`
- `score_jeune_adulte`
- `score_junior`
- `score_actifs`

using your weights from algo.py.

---

## Typical Gold outputs for your project

- `gold_services_by_arrondissement.parquet`
- `gold_population_by_arrondissement.parquet`
- `gold_environment_by_arrondissement.parquet`
- `gold_pafs_scores.parquet`

---

## What not to do in Gold

Avoid doing raw cleaning here:
- no messy renaming
- no heavy missing-value cleaning
- no raw filtering logic

That should already be done in **Silver**.

---

## Recommended order

1. load Silver parquet files  
2. aggregate by arrondissement  
3. compute thematic indicators  
4. normalize metrics  
5. calculate final profile scores  
6. save final Gold tables

---

with all the indicators merged into one final scoring table.
